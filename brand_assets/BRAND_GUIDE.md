# Flowbuild Brand Guide

## Company
- **Name:** flowbuild
- **Domain:** flowbuild.ca
- **Type:** Business automation agency
- **Markets:** London ON, Toronto ON, Vancouver BC, Guadalajara MX

## Logo
- **File:** flowbuild_logo.png
- **Style:** Teal workflow-node icon (connected dots) + serif wordmark, all lowercase

## Colors
- **Primary Teal:** #00B4B0
- **Black (text/wordmark):** #1A1A1A
- **White (backgrounds):** #FFFFFF
- **Dark background variant:** #0D1117

## Typography
- **Headings:** High-contrast serif (Tiempos Headline, Freight Display, or similar)
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans, or similar)
- **Logo wordmark:** Serif, lowercase, medium weight

## Brand Voice
- Professional but approachable
- Clear, no jargon — speaks to SMB owners, not developers
- Confident without being salesy
- Bilingual capability (English + Spanish)
