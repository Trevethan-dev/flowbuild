The process

Simple from
day one.

We keep the process simple. You show us what's slowing you down, and we build a system that fixes it. Usually in under two weeks.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a short call to understand your business: where time is being lost, what tools you use, and what success looks like for you.

02

Workflow Audit

We map your current process and identify the tasks that can be automated, simplified, or improved. You get a clear plan with expected results.

03

Build & Test

Our team builds your automations and runs them through real scenarios before anything goes live. You review, approve, and we refine until it's perfect.

04

Launch & Ongoing Support

After launch, we help you monitor the system, make improvements, and adjust it as your business grows.
