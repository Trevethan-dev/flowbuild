The problem

By the time you call back,
they've moved on.

When someone fills out a form on your website, requests a quote, or calls in an inquiry, that moment of interest is the peak of their intent. They are ready to buy — right now. Research consistently shows that contacting a lead within five minutes makes you up to ten times more likely to convert them compared to waiting thirty minutes. And yet the average business takes 47 hours to follow up.

By that point, the prospect has already talked to two of your competitors, forgot they even reached out, or solved the problem themselves. You didn't lose that lead because your price was wrong or your service was bad. You lost it because you were slow.

This isn't about working faster. It's about building a system that responds instantly — every single time, for every single lead, without anyone on your team lifting a finger.

How it works

From form submit
to replied — in seconds.

01

Lead captured

The moment a form is submitted — on your website, a landing page, or a third-party platform — the automation fires. Name, contact info, and any intake fields are captured immediately.

02

Qualified automatically

The system checks the lead against your criteria — budget, location, service type, or any other filter. Hot leads get priority routing. Unqualified leads get a polite response that doesn't waste your team's time.

03

Routed to the right person

Based on the lead's profile, it's assigned to the right rep, territory, or department — with a notification that includes everything they need to know before picking up the phone.

04

Personalized reply sent instantly

While routing happens internally, the prospect receives a personalized message — by email, SMS, or both — that feels human. It acknowledges their inquiry, sets expectations, and keeps them warm until your team connects.
