How It Works

From form submit to replied — in seconds.

Lead captured

The moment a form is submitted — on your website, landing page, or any platform — the automation fires. Name, contact info, and intake fields are captured immediately.

Qualified automatically

The system checks the lead against your criteria — budget, location, service type. Hot leads get priority routing; unqualified leads get a polite response that doesn't waste your team's time.

Routed to the right person

Based on the lead's profile, it's assigned to the right rep or territory — with a notification that includes everything they need before picking up the phone.

Personalized reply sent instantly

While routing happens internally, the prospect receives a personalized message — by email, SMS, or both — that feels human and keeps them warm until your team connects.
