The problem

You're sitting on a goldmine
you've completely
forgotten about.

Every business that's been around for any amount of time has the same thing: a CRM full of contacts who showed real interest at some point, and then went quiet. Past customers who haven't come back. Newsletter subscribers who never bought. People who started a free trial and disappeared. Leads who had a great first conversation and then just stopped responding.

These people aren't strangers. They already know your business. They already had some level of interest. They don't need to be sold on who you are — they just need a reason to restart the conversation. That's a very different (and much cheaper) problem to solve than finding new leads from scratch.

No new ad spend. No new lead generation. Just a smart, automated campaign to your existing database that turns dormant contacts into active opportunities.

How it works

From forgotten contact
to warm lead.

01

Pull from your database

We connect to your CRM, email list, or any other system where your contacts live. All dormant contacts are identified — past customers, old leads, trial drop-offs, cold subscribers.

02

Segmented by dropout stage

Every contact gets categorized by where they fell off. Someone who was a paying customer gets a very different message than someone who filled out a form and never replied. The system handles this automatically.

03

Personalized outreach sent

Each segment receives a tailored message — acknowledging the relationship, offering something relevant, and making it easy to say yes. Not a mass blast. A campaign that reads like a personal note.

04

Responders qualified automatically

When someone replies with interest, the system asks the right qualifying questions and determines if they're a good fit — before any human time is invested.

05

Warm leads handed to sales

Qualified, interested contacts are passed to your team as warm leads — with full context of the conversation and why they re-engaged. All the heavy lifting is already done.
