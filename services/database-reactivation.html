How It Works

From dormant database to live pipeline.

Pull from your database

We connect to your CRM, email list, or any system where contacts live. All dormant contacts are identified — past customers, old leads, trial drop-offs, cold subscribers.

Segmented by dropout stage

Every contact gets categorized by where they fell off. A past customer gets a very different message than someone who filled out a form and never replied.

Personalized outreach sent

Each segment receives a tailored message — acknowledging the relationship, offering something relevant, and making it easy to say yes. Not a mass blast.

Responders qualified automatically

When someone replies with interest, the system asks qualifying questions and determines fit — before any human time is invested.

Warm leads handed to sales

Qualified contacts are passed to your team with full context of the conversation and why they re-engaged. All the heavy lifting is done.
