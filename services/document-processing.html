The problem

Hours lost every day
to work a computer
could do.

Invoices come in. Someone opens them, reads the details, types the vendor name into one system, the amount into another, checks it against something else, and then moves on to the next one. Multiply that by every document your team handles every day. It adds up to a staggering amount of time — spent on work that requires zero judgment, zero creativity, and zero expertise.

And because it's repetitive, manual, and tedious, it's also where errors happen. A wrong digit in an amount. A misclassified expense. A vendor entered slightly differently than last time. Small mistakes that create real problems downstream.

Document processing automation is one of the most predictable and high-ROI automations we build. No AI agent required — just clean, reliable logic that handles the flow of data exactly as it should.

How it works

45 minutes becomes
2 minutes.

01

Document arrives

The workflow triggers the moment a new document lands — via email, a shared folder, an upload portal, or any other source your business already uses.

02

Data extracted

The automation pulls the relevant fields — vendor name, invoice number, date, line items, totals, tax, and anything else you need — cleanly and consistently every time.

03

Validated & checked

The extracted data is checked against your chart of accounts, approved vendor list, or any other reference. Duplicates are flagged. Anomalies are surfaced. Everything else moves forward automatically.

04

Routed to the right place

Clean data gets pushed exactly where it needs to go — your accounting software, project management tool, approval queue, or all three. No copy-pasting.

05

Human reviews in 2 minutes

A team member gets a clean summary to approve or flag. Everything is pre-filled. They're reviewing, not entering. What used to take 45 minutes now takes two.
