The Problem

Hours lost every day to work a computer could do.

Invoices arrive. Someone opens them, reads the details, types the vendor name into one system and the amount into another, checks it against something else, then moves on to the next one. Multiply that by every document your team handles daily — it adds up to a staggering amount of time spent on work that requires zero judgment or expertise.

And because it's repetitive and tedious, it's also where errors happen. A wrong digit. A misclassified expense. A vendor entered slightly differently than last time. Small mistakes that create real problems downstream.

Document processing automation is one of the most predictable and high-ROI automations we build. No AI agent required — just clean, reliable logic that handles data flow exactly as it should.

How It Works

Document in. Clean data out.

Document arrives

The workflow triggers the moment a new document lands — via email, shared folder, or upload portal. Any source your business already uses.

Data extracted

The automation pulls the relevant fields — vendor name, invoice number, date, line items, totals, tax — cleanly and consistently every time.

Validated & checked

Extracted data is checked against your chart of accounts or approved vendor list. Duplicates are flagged. Anomalies are surfaced. Everything else moves forward automatically.

Routed to the right place

Clean data gets pushed exactly where it needs to go — your accounting software, project management tool, or approval queue. No copy-pasting.

Human reviews in 2 minutes

A team member gets a clean summary to approve or flag. Everything is pre-filled. They're reviewing, not entering.
