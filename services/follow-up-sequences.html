The Problem

Warm leads go cold because nobody followed up enough.

Businesses spend a lot of money acquiring leads. A prospect fills out a form, books a call, or attends a webinar. The business follows up once, maybe twice, and then moves on. Meanwhile, the prospect was genuinely interested — they just needed more time, or one more touchpoint before they were ready to say yes.

Research shows 80% of sales require more than five follow-ups. Most sales teams stop after one or two. That gap is where revenue disappears — and those leads were already warm. You'd done the hard work of getting their attention.

A well-built follow-up sequence is personalized at every step, pulls context from your CRM, and stops automatically the moment someone replies or books. It never feels like spam because it isn't.

How It Works

From first touch to conversion — automatically.

Trigger event fires

A lead fills out a form, books a call, or hits any trigger you define. The sequence kicks off within 60 seconds — while they're still thinking about you.

First message: instant & personal

A personalized message goes out immediately, referencing what they did and what comes next. It sounds like a person wrote it — because the template was written by one.

3–5 touch points over two weeks

The system sends a series of value-driven follow-ups spaced strategically, each pulling fresh context from your CRM so they never feel templated.

Sequence stops on response

The moment someone replies or books a call, the sequence halts automatically. No awkward double-messages. No leads followed up after they've already said yes.

Sales team notified with full context

When a lead engages, your team gets notified with the full conversation thread, their profile, what they clicked, and what they said.
