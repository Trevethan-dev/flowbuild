About Flowbuild

Built by someone who got tired of doing the same work twice.

FlowBuild is a business automation agency based in Canada. We design and build custom workflows that handle the repetitive stuff so you and your team can spend time on the work that actually matters.

The Short Version

I started my first company in university. A Spanish-language wellness app called Despertar. We grew it to thousands of users, ran content across every platform, and learned firsthand what happens when a growing business runs on manual processes. Every hour we spent copying data, sending follow-ups by hand, and updating spreadsheets was an hour we weren't spending on the product.

So I started building systems to handle it. Lead responses, reporting, content pipelines, onboarding flows. Anything that happened more than once, I automated. The impact was immediate. And once I saw how much time it freed up, I realized every small business I knew had the same problem.
